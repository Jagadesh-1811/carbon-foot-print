Privacy Policy

Planetary Data Privacy Protocol — Last Updated June 2026

01 Information We Collect

We collect profile details (display name, email address) and utility, shopping, or transit carbon telemetry metrics you intentionally record. No raw coordinates or biometric details are harvested without explicit permission.

02 Data Sharing

EcoTrace does not distribute, rent, or trade your carbon output telemetry to commercial agencies. Aggregate, non-identifiable regional carbon savings statistics are used to show community impact on our public maps.

03 Telemetry Security

All telemetry, including goal metrics and active session states, is stored in protected database collections. We execute periodic security sweeps to confirm the integrity of user profile directories.
